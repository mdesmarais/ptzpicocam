class ptzsimcam.robot_camera.RobotCamera(main_link, pan_joint_index, tilt_joint_index)¶

Represents a camera that uses the VISCA protocol.

Creates a new robot camera in a simulation.

A robot camera is made of 2 joints.

Parameters

  • main_link – root link in the associated urdf

  • pan_joint_index (int) – index of the first joint

  • tilt_joint_index (int) – index of the second joint
